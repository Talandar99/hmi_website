Achievements

1. Two grants from the Ministry of Science and Higher Education

Finalists of the ministerial competition titled: "Student Scientific Circles Create Innovations", project title: “Artificial Intelligence in Industrial Applications”, 2021. Title of the second grant: "Digital Twin - Safe Stage Rotor Project", currently being implemented in 2025.

2. Finalists of awards in the years:

➤2024 awarded to Eng. Maciej Kazior the II degree award in the XI edition of the competition for the best engineering thesis, organized by Polish Electrical Engineers Association Gdańsk Branch.
➤2023 The Minister of Infrastructure awarded to Eng. Piotr Sokół the prize for the best shipboard electro-automatics
➤2020 nationwide competition “Polish PLC Programmers Championships”: - I – Polish vice-champion in the Expert category for Eng. Dawid Trzciński

3. Organization of workshops

Organizing popular science workshops on programming controllers for people with disabilities from “House of Dreams” 2024 and workshops titled “Industry 4.0” in collaboration with Mr. Sasa Simov, international Director of World Skills and Director of FESTO DIDACTIC in 2023.

4. Scientific Activity

Participation in conferences, writing scientific publications since 2020, a total of 19 publications have been written.

5. Collaboration with Industry and Promotion of Alma Mater

Collaboration with industry and engaging in the promotion of one's own Alma Mater, including participation in the scientific circles festival Sciencecom 2024. Additionally, we are facilitating the process of familiarizing students with companies in the land and marine automation industry. Over the past three years, we have established cooperation with companies: Sii, Automaze, Robotpol, WiRan.
